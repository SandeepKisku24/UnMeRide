CREDIT SYSTEM

What differentiates us from others out there?


Our App works on a credit-based system.  With each ride given the consumer will receive a certain number of credit points to his account. On the other hand, if the consumer is opting for a ride then a certain number of credit points will be deducted from his account for each ride As a starter, 100 credit points will be given to all the customers who make an account in our app If the credit points in one account is exhausted then the consumer should remit a certain amount to buy  credit points


OUR PROFIT:-

Let the credit points earned by the driver be m per km. Let the credit points used by the user be n per km. Let the traveled distance be k km. So the total profit earned is (n-m)*k. In the above case, the profit will be = (5-4)*2.6 = 3 credit points earned.
